FAQ

Common questions

Do I need technical knowledge to use this? +
Not at all. We handle the entire setup for you. Once it's running, managing the AI is as simple as a regular messaging app — you view conversations, update your documents, and change settings through a visual dashboard.
What if the AI says something wrong? +
The AI only uses information from your approved knowledge base — it won't make things up. For any question it can't confidently answer, it automatically passes the conversation to your team with a notification. You always stay in control.
How long does it take to set up? +
Typically 5–7 business days from the moment you share your materials. We configure, train, and test everything. You approve the result before going live.
What are the ongoing costs after the $950 setup? +
The $950 is a one-time installation fee. After that, you pay directly for the AI model usage (typically $10–50/month through OpenAI or Google, depending on your conversation volume) and your hosting (Railway or similar, ~$5–20/month). No markup, no subscriptions to us.
Can I update the AI's knowledge after setup? +
Yes. You have a simple admin panel where you can upload new documents, update prices, or change your FAQ at any time. The AI re-learns from new materials within minutes.
What's included in custom development? +
Custom development covers special requirements beyond the standard setup — for example, connecting the AI to your booking system, CRM, inventory database, or payment tools. We discuss your needs and provide an individual quote.
