FAQ

Common questions

Why pay once instead of monthly? +
Most AI tools charge $50–500/month forever. We charge $950 once and you own everything — the setup, the knowledge base, the agents. Your ongoing costs are just your API keys (~$10–50/month depending on volume), paid directly to OpenAI/Google. No markup. No lock-in.
Can I run agents for multiple businesses from one account? +
Yes. You can create as many AI agents as you need, each with a different persona, knowledge base, and connected channel. This is ideal for agencies, multi-brand businesses, or anyone managing multiple projects from one dashboard.
What if the AI gives a wrong answer? +
The AI only uses information from your approved knowledge base — it won't make things up. For any question it can't confidently answer, it automatically passes the conversation to your team. You always stay in control with custom escalation rules.
Can I update the AI's knowledge after setup? +
Yes. You have a simple admin panel where you can upload new documents, update prices, or change your FAQ at any time. The AI re-learns from new materials within minutes.
What custom integrations are possible? +
We can connect the AI to almost any system — your booking platform, CRM, inventory database, payment tools, or internal ERP. We discuss your specific needs and build the integration as part of a custom development package.
